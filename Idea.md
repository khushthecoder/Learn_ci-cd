# Veloura – Gen-Z Fashion E-Commerce Platform

## Overview
Veloura is a next-gen e-commerce platform built for Gen-Z fashion lovers, offering trendy **t-shirts, shirts, hoodies, and accessories**.  
The platform focuses on bold aesthetics, lightning-fast browsing, and a seamless mobile-first shopping experience.

---

## Problem Statement
- Gen-Z consumers demand trendy, affordable, and expressive fashion.
- Most e-commerce platforms feel cluttered, outdated, and generic.
- Independent and small fashion brands lack a dedicated platform to reach their audience.

---

## Proposed Solution
Veloura enables users to:
- Discover and shop trending t-shirts, shirts, and curated fashion drops.
- Browse Gen-Z inspired collections with a clean, modern UI.
- Experience a fast, intuitive, and distraction-free checkout flow.

---

## Key Features

### 1. Product Catalog (Core)
- Browse t-shirts, shirts, and more by category.
- Smart filters for size, color, price range, and style tags.
- High-quality product imagery with zoom support.

### 2. Product Details
- Multiple product images with swipe gallery.
- Size guide and real-time stock availability.
- Product descriptions and customer reviews.

### 3. Cart & Checkout
- Add, update, and remove items from cart.
- Select size, color variant, and quantity.
- Simple and secure checkout with order summary.

### 4. User Authentication
- User signup and login with email.
- Order history and tracking.
- Profile and address management.

### 5. Admin Dashboard
- Add, update, and delete products.
- Manage inventory, stock levels, and pricing.
- View, process, and manage customer orders.

### 6. Smart Search & Recommendations
- Keyword-based product search with auto-suggestions.
- Personalized product recommendations based on browsing history.
- Trending and most popular items section on the homepage.

### 7. Dark Mode & Theme Toggle
- Built-in dark mode for a premium browsing experience.
- User preference saved across sessions.
- Smooth theme transition animations.

---

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** React.js / Next.js  
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js  
- **Database:** SQLite3 or PostgreSQL with Prisma ORM  
- **Deployment:** Vercel (Frontend), Render (Backend)

---

## Unique Value Proposition
- Gen-Z focused UI/UX with bold, expressive design language.
- Trend-driven and seasonally curated clothing collections.
- Clean, responsive, and accessible design across all devices.
- Easily scalable architecture for future growth.
